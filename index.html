After 8 rounds of the game, the person who has it will lose. Therefore, the players have to make sure they don't have the bomb card. But, the bomb card cannot be discarded during the odd number rounds. It can only be discarded by the even number rounds. +, One ultimate way to win is by guessing who has the bomb card with the bet card, but that may lead to the lose+ * One odd case is that no one gets the bomb card. In this case, the person that has the highest number in their hand, or has the bonus card wins. The bonus card: *The bonus card is a special card. It can be used in the odd number rounds, round 1, 3, 5, and 7 and it allows you to throw away one more card with it, and take back two cards with it from the mixed deck. This way, you would have less possibility of getting the bomb. furthermore, if you do not want it used this way, the bonus card can also be used in the even number round, and you are able to excahnge 2 times with any player, and it can be the same player. The bet card: The bet card can be used at the very end. The person that has the bet card can guess who has the bomb card, then if the person gets the guess right, then the person will become the ultimate winner. However, if the person gets the guess wrong, the person will become the person with the bomb. And he or she would lose. Using this bet card isn't a must but you can choose to take the risk if you want to. If you don't, and you do not have a bomb card, it would be a tie between you and the other person who doesn't have the bomb Discarding cards: When discarding cards, You are able to discard any cards that you have in your hands, however, you are not able to discard the bomb card.
