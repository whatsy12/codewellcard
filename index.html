player 3 to 4
how to play:
1.each player gets 3 cards from the mixed deck
2.the player take turns to put their cards on the deposit deck.
in the first round, the players are able to put down one card,
and it can be the 1 to 8 cards.
3.after the first round of putting down cards, in the
second round,the players have to exchange their cards once.
on the next turn players have to exchange cards.
4.put down card-odd nubers,exchange-even numbers.
5.play until 8 and the player that has bet could guess who has the bomb.

how to win:
The main goal of this game is to not get the bomb card. After 8
rounds of the game, the person who has it will lose. Therefore, the
players have to make sure they don't have the bomb card. But, the
bomb card cannot be discarded during the odd number rounds. It can
only be discarded by the even number rounds. +, One ultimate way to
win is by guessing who has the bomb card with the bet card, but that may lead to the lose+
* One odd case is that no one gets the bomb card. In this case,
the person that has the highest number in their hand, or has the
bonus card wins.

The bonus card:
*The bonus card is a special card. It can be used in the odd number
rounds, round 1, 3, 5, and 7 and it allows you to throw away one more
card with it, and take back two cards with it from the mixed deck. This way,
you would have less possibility of getting the bomb. furthermore, if
you do not want it used this way, the bonus card can also be used in
the even number round, and you are able to excahnge 2 times with
any player, and it can be the same player.

The bet card:
The bet card can be used at the very end. The person that has the
bet card can guess who has the bomb card, then if the person gets
the guess right, then the person will become the ultimate winner.
However, if the person gets the guess wrong, the person will become the
person with the bomb. And he or she would lose. Using this bet card
isn't a must but you can choose to take the risk if you want to. If you don't, and
you do not have a bomb card, it would be a tie between you and the
other person who doesn't have the bomb

Discarding cards:
When discarding cards, You are able to discard any cards that you
have in your hands, however, you are not able to discard the bomb
card. And after the discard of one card, you must take a new card
from the mixed deck
